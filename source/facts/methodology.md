## Method (the deep-search)

1. **Scan what's genuinely new** on the themes since the last pass (web search + deep-research
   capabilities when available).
2. **Go to the original source**, not a secondary summary; note the exact figure, its context, and
   where it appears (a URL, or a document name + page number).
3. **Prefer a source that states its own methodology.**
4. **Never bank the unverifiable** — say so rather than guess.
5. **Check `source/facts/` for a near-duplicate** before creating a file; enrich rather than duplicate.
6. **Bank each real hit** per the `research` skill's convention. Every third-party figure needs a
   check before it ships publicly.
